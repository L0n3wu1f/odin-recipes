Krumkake

Description

Norwegian Krumkake is a flavorful, customizable, wafer-like cookie. You can top them with your favorite fillings, dip them in melted chocolate, make homemade ice cream cones, or just add whipped cream. Delish!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teasponn vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter flavoring (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat krumkake iron on stove over medium heat, may also use an electric krumkake or pizzelle iron.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar in a bowl. Add the eggs, on at a time, and mix well using a spoon.
  3. Pour in the milk, flour, vanilla, and butter flavoring if desired; mix well.
  4. Place a teaspoon of batter on the preheated iron and press together.
  5. Cook until browned, about 30 seconds each side depending on heat.
  6. Remove from the iron and quickly roll up around a stick or cone before they harden.
  7. Enjoy!
